WHAT ARE FIDGETERS?

Fideters are a range of products that are designed to be played with to increase focus and attention. Fidgeting, fiddling, or as teachers call it, "stop that", has been demonstrated to increase productivity and attention span.

WHO ARE FIDGETERS FOR?

Anyone. And their mum.

WHAT ARE THE PRODUCTS MADE FROM?

Materials vary between the different products, however the common component in all three is Polyurethane (PUR) plastic. The Cowbone is entirely made from Polyurethane, the Baton is mostly Polyurethane, but has Silicone caps, and the Pennies are made from enamel painted metal with Polyurethane discs on either side.
If you have more questions about the materials we use, you can contact us at hello@fidgeters.co.uk or tweet us at @fidgetermakes.

HOW CAN I STAY INFORMED?

We are on Instagram, Twitter and Facebook, if you want stay casually informed. However, if you really consider yourself a die-hard fan, you can subscribe to our newsletter, and we'll send you news, offers and updates before anyone else.

WHAT DO I DO IF MY FIDGETER HAS A DEFECT? WHAT DO I DO IF I WANT TO RETURN MY FIDGETER?

If your fidgeter has a defect, you can send it back to us, and we will replace it at no extra cost. If you don't like it, but there is no defect, you can also return it to us within 2 months of placing the original order. If you do this, we can give you a refund or offer to give you a different product for free.
For advice on returns + refunds, and other product enquiries, you can email us hello@fidgeters.co.uk

HOW MANY DIFFERENT FIDGETERS ARE THERE?

Currently there are only 3 fidgeters available - the Cowbone, a unique organic shape in hard resin plastic; the Baton, a weighted rod ideal for twirling and wiggling; the Pennies, a rotating dynamic Fidgeter with spinning parts. All of the products in our range have been carefully designed to fit the hand perfectly at the same as rejecting states of rest, to inspire focus in motion. Each product is inspired by a different way that people fidget, which is why the range will be expanding shortly.
