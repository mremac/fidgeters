About Paragraph

Brainstorming doesn't have to be a headache. Paying attention shouldn't be taxing. Problem solving should be more daydream and less nightmare
- Work should be less work and more play.
Fidgeters are objects designed to be played with in order to stimulate the creative process. Each one is inspired by a different way that people like to fidget.

Cowbone

The Cowbone is a single piece of solid plastic, cast with setting resin in a silicone mold. The shape, designed to nicely fit the hand while encouraging motion, is inspired by the natural shape of vertebrae.

Baton

The Baton is a weighted rod, perfect for twirling between fingers. It is made from steel and food-safe silicon, ideal for chewing on during a brainstorming session.

Pennies

The Pennies - You can go to 5 or 6 stores or just one. I can't believe you've done this.
